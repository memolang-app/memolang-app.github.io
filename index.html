Leitner approach

With the Leitner approach you'll keep reviewing questions in specific interval. At first you review every day but if you remember the answer this interval keeps growing until you finally master the answer. However if you forget the answer, you go back to reviewing it everyday.

Riding the clouds

Your data is stored on the cloud. So you can study on multiple devices, be it a browser on your device, our app installed on your android phone or on your iPhone.

Multiple subjects

Simply enter the app with your email. Create as many study subjects as you want and insert flash cards in them. Then study the flash cards with our app.
